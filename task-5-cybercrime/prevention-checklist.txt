Stay Safe Online Checklist
✔ Do not share OTP, passwords, or bank details with anyone
✔ Always check the website URL before entering details
✔ Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
✔ Use strong and unique passwords
✔ Enable two-factor authentication on accounts
✔ Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or payments
✔ Verify messages before trusting them
✔ Regularly update your apps and devices
